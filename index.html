About Us

The company was founded in 1908 by the Papatsa brothers, with the main activity being the production of industrial agricultural products. At the beginning of the 1950s, it passed into the hands of Paraschos Tsalapataras who took care of its expansion and modernization.

In 1976, it relocated to a privately owned, fully equipped industrial site in Ionia, Thessaloniki, where it continues to manufacture and import all types of agricultural machinery to this day.

In 2007, Paraschos Tsalapataras was honored by E.KA.GE.M as a pioneer in the development of the agricultural machinery industry. Since 2009, the arduous work of Mr. Tsalapataras has been continued by his partner of ten years, Stylianos Arvithis, as well as his children Anestis Perisinakis, Lamprini, and Athanasios. Consistent with its 100-year tradition, the company's goal always remains the excellent service of the Greek farmer.
